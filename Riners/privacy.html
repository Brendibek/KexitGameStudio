2. Information we collect

Account & authentication data

When you sign in using Google Play Games Services we may receive:

  • Google Play Games Services user identifier
  • Basic profile information permitted by Google Play

Gameplay and multiplayer data

To operate multiplayer features we collect:

  • Matchmaking and session data
  • Gameplay progress and actions
  • Player identifiers required to maintain multiplayer sessions

Chat data (User Generated Content)

Players may communicate using in-game text chat.

We collect and store:

  • Messages sent through the in-game chat
  • Sender identifiers and timestamps

Chat messages may be stored and retained on our servers to:

  • Provide communication between players
  • Moderate player behavior
  • Investigate cheating, abuse, or rule violations
  • Improve safety and the overall player experience

Chat messages may be reviewed by automated systems or moderators.

Technical and device data

We may collect:

  • Device type and operating system
  • IP address
  • App version
  • Crash logs and diagnostics

This information helps maintain stability and security of the game.

Purchase information

If you make in-app purchases:

  • Transactions are processed through Google Play Billing
  • We may receive confirmation of purchases and related transaction metadata

We do not receive or store payment card information.

3. How we use information

We use collected data to:

  • Operate the game and multiplayer services
  • Enable player communication through chat
  • Moderate behavior and enforce rules
  • Detect cheating, fraud, and abuse
  • Process in-app purchases
  • Maintain security and stability
  • Improve gameplay experience
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Legal basis for processing (GDPR)

Where applicable under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), we process personal data based on:

  • Legitimate interests – maintaining security, preventing cheating, and moderating user behavior
  • Legal obligations – complying with applicable laws and regulations

To maintain a safe environment:
